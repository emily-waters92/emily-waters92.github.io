My learning plan

What is your long-term goal or career pathway?
I would love to become a website designer and to work my way up in a company to a leadership role. To be able to work from home would be a bonus too as my daughter has a full-on dance schedule!

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning.
My strengths would be my work ethic – I will go and go until I get what I need to get done, done but that could also be a limitation as I could be prone to burnout. Another limitation is I tend to get distracted easily (hopefully this will change when my daughter goes back to school). I am a problem solver, so that is definitely a strength of mine, being a single Mum I have had to adapt and figure it out or it won’t get done as I can’t afford to pay any one to do it!

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?
Learning how to stop and meditate or turn off my brain. I think it will be really beneficial to my focus (or lack of!). I really enjoyed looking into my values, strengths and limitations too so more of that sort of thing where we do a deep dive into our psyche as I believe that this will help me to develop skills where I may be limited.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.
I will stop and take regular breaks so as not to get stressed out or burnt out. I will present the best of myself to facilitators, industry and community representatives as I myself am a representative of Dev Academy. I will change it up if I am feeling stuck or stressed – go for a walk outside, go and get a coffee, change up the scenery. I will put my best effort in everyday and give my all to this programme, my peers and my facilitators.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way.
I will continue to use Toggl Track so that I can see how long I am spending on certain projects, set myself timebox goals and use the Pomodoro timer. I will reach out for help on Discord to my cohort peers when I need help with something that is taking me past my timebox goal.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team.
Patience and kindness while we are learning this new language. To be willing to help when we are stuck and maybe offer up a bit of advice, support or humour when needed!
