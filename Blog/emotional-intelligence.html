What is emotional intelligence

“Emotional intelligence (EI) is the ability to perceive, interpret, demonstrate, control, evaluate, and use emotions to communicate with and relate to others effectively and constructively.”
-verywellmind.com

Emotional intelligence is to be able to accurately perceive the emotion/s (can include facial expressions and body language), to be able to understand why we/they are feeling the emotion and empathize, and to be able to manage and regulate emotions – as well as being able to communicate these successfully.
How is EQ different to IQ?
IQ is used to determine academic abilities such as one’s ability to solve problems and understand or convey compound ideas. EQ measures one’ social and emotional capability.

Why is EQ important?
EQ is important as it helps us to be better humans being by being more self and socially aware. It is a key factor in building relationships, defusing stress and conflict within ourselves and with others and helps us to be more empathetic and compassionate which in turn, makes for a kinder person.
